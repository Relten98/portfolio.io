As for my other iterests and skills besides web design, I also take great joy in designing levels and maps for various video games. To me, level design is just an awesome thing, as it is a balance between how you present the level itself, along with how the player can interpret those elements, and incorporate these elements into their own play, thus impacting their own overall enjoyment of the final product.

Taking this aspect of interest into account, it perhaps should be somewhat obvious that this background in level design that I have, is perhaps somewhat present in even the web pages that I design, as I always had enjoyed the interactive element of websites, and I feel that interactivity is the key towards forging excellent user experiences.
